Quantitative Management

Applying mathematical models to business decision making. Also known as Management Science or Operations Research.

Linear Programming

Optimization of resources. Maximizing profit or minimizing cost under constraints.

Decision Theory

Making choices under risk (probabilities known) and uncertainty (probabilities unknown).

Game Theory

Strategic interactions between competitors. Nash Equilibrium and Prisoner's Dilemma.

Operations Research

Transportation, Assignment, and Inventory models (EOQ).
